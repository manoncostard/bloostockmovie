BLOODSTOCK

A reckoning of rural America and a rare glimpse of equestrian culture, BLOODSTOCK offers it's audience a "Midwestern"

The Story

After serving two years in prison, DANIEL, a once promising race horse trainer turned back of the track drug-dealer, is torn between returning to his criminal ways under his nefarious boss, Wagner, or reconciling with his ailing parents.  His battles with addiction are either amplified or alleviated by his star-crossed relationship with RED, an absolute barn burner of a woman, his childhood love, and Wagner's daughter. Daniel must navigate the turbulent waters of the midwest thoroughbred horse racing industry as a true underdog,  while he strives for redemption and helps his ailing father achieve his life's goal of racing glory.
